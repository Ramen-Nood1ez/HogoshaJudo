Instructors

Jerry L. Cypert (Supervising Instructor)- a fifth (5th) degree Black Belt certified by USA Judo – the certification body for the United States Olympic Committee (USOC). Sensei Jerry began Judo in high school where Judo was a letter sport. Sensei Cypert, an International Level Coach, and Vision Impaired Coach. Sensei Jerry has coached at the USJF Junior National Championships, AM-CAN International Judo Challenge, National Scholastics Championships, Junior Olympics International Championships, and the US Open. Sensei Cypert is a National level Referee wtih IPSI Certification, and has refereed throughout the US.

Jerry is a 1974 Graduate of UW - Oshkosh, where he earned his bachelor degree majoring in Political Science and minoring in Speech, 1976 Graudate of UW - Milwaukee, where he earned his Masters degree in Social Work (MSW). He also is certified by the Academy of Social Workers (ASW).

Darlene S. Cypert (Head Instructor)-a Third (3rd) degree Black Belt certified by USA Judo. Sensei Darlene started Judo in 2000. Darlene is a 2007 Graduate of Concordia University - Mequon, where she earned her Bachelor degree in Management of Criminal Justice, and is a retired Correctional Sergeant for the Wisconsin Department of Corrections. Sensei Darlene is the business manager of Hogosha Judo llc and the lead coach for: local, state and regional tournaments. She is a National Level Coach.

Josh Cypert (Associate Instructor) - a Third (3rd) degree Black Belt certified by USA Judo. Sensei Josh is a National Level Coach, National Referee, an IPSI certified Coach and Referee.

Sensei Josh, a national competitor, has competed at the following higher level events: USJF Junior National Championships, AM-CAN International Judo Challenge, National Scholastics Championships, Junior Olympics International Championships, and the US Open.

Sensei Josh (with his brother Mike) captured a Bronze at the USJF Junior National Kata Championships and the medal was presented by Keiko Fukuda Sensei.

Josh is a 2017 Graduate of UW - Whitewater, where he earned his bachelor degree in Criminal Justice. Josh is a former 2nd Lieutenant in the United States Army, branching in Field Artillery. Sensei Josh is also the former Judo instructor at Elkhorn Flying Dragons in Elkhorn, WI.

Mike Cypert (Associate Instructor) - a Third (3rd) degree Black Belt certified by USA Judo. Sensei Mike is a National Level Coach and Regional Referee.

Sensei Mike, a national competitor, has competed at the following higher level events: USJF Junior National Championships, AM-CAN International Judo Challenge, National Scholastics Championships, Junior Olympics International Championships, and the US Open.

Sensei Mike (with his brother Josh) captured a Bronze at the USJF Junior National Kata Championships and the medal was presented by Keiko Fukuda Sensei.

Mike is a 2015 Graduate of UW – Madison, where he earned his Bachelor Degree (double major) in East Asian Studies, Chinese and Criminal Justice. Mike is a police officer for the city of Juneau police department.
